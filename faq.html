What is DermAI?

DermAI is an experimental chatbot for skin cancer. The bot is able to answer relative questions, use AI to detect skin cancer, and find nearby doctors based on the user's location.

How accurate is the skin cancer detection?

The accuracy of skin cancer detection may vary based on various factors. While DermAI utilizes advanced AI algorithms, it's important to consult a medical professional for an accurate diagnosis. Also, note that at the moment DermAI can only detect Dermatofibroma, Melanocytic Nevi, Vascular lesions or say the image does not show signs of skin cancer.

Can I trust the doctor recommendations provided by DermAI?

DermAI provides doctor recommendations based on the user's location, but it's always recommended to do your own research and consider other factors such as reviews and qualifications before making a decision.
